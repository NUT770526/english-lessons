Mess or Clutter?

Look at the mini-picture and sentence. Choose the correct word.

MESS 😵‍💫

Mess = dirty, untidy, chaotic.

Things are all over the place. It looks bad or hard to use.

Example: There is a mess in the kitchen.

CLUTTER 📚

Clutter = too many things in one place.

It may be clean, but there is too much stuff.

Example: There is too much clutter on my desk.
