GERUND: verb + -ing

Герундій після enjoy, finish, keep on, go on, give up

Правило: після дієслів enjoy, finish, keep on, go on, give up наступне дієслово вживається із закінченням -ing.
I enjoy learning English. — Я люблю вивчати англійську.
She finished writing the report. — Вона закінчила писати звіт.
enjoy + -ing finish + -ing keep on + -ing go on + -ing give up + -ing

1. Оберіть правильну форму дієслова

Після виділених дієслів оберіть форму з -ing.
I enjoy ___ English after work.
She finished ___ the monthly report.
Please keep on ___ on the new project.
He went on ___ after the meeting ended.
The manager gave up ___ every small mistake.
We enjoy ___ pleasant conversations during lunch.
She finished ___ her job and went home.
The company keeps on ___ a profit every year.
He went on ___ promises that he could not keep.
Do not give up ___ after one mistake.

2. Поставте дієслово у форму герундія

Напишіть правильну форму дієслова із закінченням -ing.
I enjoy (learn) new business vocabulary.
She finished (write) the email.
We keep on (work) on the same project.
He went on (talk) after the presentation.
They gave up (smoke) last year.
My manager enjoys (have) conversations with clients.
She finished (do) her job before lunch.
The company keeps on (make) a profit.
He gave up (make) the same mistake.
Please go on (read) the report.
Зверніть увагу: make → making, write → writing, have → having.

3. Виправте помилку

У кожному реченні є помилка після enjoy, finish, keep on, go on або give up. Напишіть речення правильно.
I enjoy to learn English.
She finished to write the report.
We keep on work on the project.
He went on to talk after the meeting.
They gave up to smoke last year.
She enjoys do her job carefully.
He finished make the presentation.
The company keeps on make a loss.
Please go on read the document.
Do not give up try after one mistake.
