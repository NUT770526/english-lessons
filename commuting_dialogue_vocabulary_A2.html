Commuting: Dialogue and Vocabulary

Read, listen and complete the exercise

1. Read the dialogue

Подчёркнутые слова могут быть сложными для уровня A2. Нажмите на слово, чтобы увидеть перевод, транскрипцию и прослушать произношение.
Alex: Hi Jane, how do you usually get to work?
Jane: Hi Alex, I take the bus. It stops right by my house. What about you?
Alex: I cycle. There's a great bicycle lane all the way to our office. How about our colleagues? Who takes the subway?
Jane: Oh, many from our team do. Especially those living near the train station. Who rides the bike like you from our office?
Alex: Sara and Mike do. They say it keeps them fit and avoids the peak hour rush.
Jane: Who finds driving to work better than public transport?
Alex: A few like John prefer driving for more convenience. How often does Sara cycle to work?
Jane: She cycles every day, unless it rains. Then she switches to the metro.
Alex: What do you usually do during your bus ride?
Jane: I usually listen to music or read. It's my quiet time before the busy day begins.
