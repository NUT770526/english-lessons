Create sentences

Use the prompts to write sentences with let or be allowed to.

Remember:
✅ let + person + verb
Example: My parents let me express myself.

✅ be allowed to + verb
Example: My brother doesn’t let me create chaos.
Examples:
my parents / __ / express myself / through art / in my room. → My parents let me express myself through art in my room.
my mom / not __ / let me / leave a pile of clothes / on the chair. → My mom doesn’t let me leave a pile of clothes on the chair.
