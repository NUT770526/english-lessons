Willy at the New Company

Read the dialogue and complete the exercises.

Christine: Daphne, have you met Willy? He started working here only last week.

Daphne: No, I haven’t. Who is Willy?

Christine: He is our new colleague. He worked for a really small company before.

Daphne: Why did he leave?

Christine: Because he had a lot of projects and often needed to work at the weekends.

Daphne: And what does he like doing?

Christine: He likes going to the cinema and playing video games.

Daphne: Is he married?

Christine: Yes, he is.

Daphne: Oh... I see.

Christine: Willy, are you happy to work for such a big company?

Willy: Of course, I am!

Christine: Do you like coffee, Willy?

Willy: I do.

Christine: Great. Let’s take a break and go out for a cup of coffee.
