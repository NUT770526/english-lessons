Work + in / for / with

Learn the difference and practise it step by step.

work in

place / building / industry

I work in a bank.

She works in IT.

work for

company / employer

He works for BMW.

They work for Google.

work with

people / things

I work with children.

She works with computers.

Quick tip: in = where?   |   for = which company?   |   with = with whom / what?
