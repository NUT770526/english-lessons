Mia’s Career

Vocabulary, reading and prepositions practice

Remember the difference:
work for a company
працювати на компанію
She works for a small company.
work at a place
працювати в певному місці
She works at a hospital.
work as + job
працювати кимось
She works as a doctor.
find time for + noun / -ing
знаходити час на щось
She finds time for chatting to friends.

Read the text

Listen and read. Pay attention to the highlighted phrases.

Listen to the whole text

Mia started a career at a small company. She has an interesting job where she makes websites. Everyone at her company is really friendly.

Mia is never unemployed because she always has projects to work on. Sometimes, Mia even works at the weekends when there are too many projects. But still she finds time for chatting to her friends and going to concerts!

One day, Mia had a promotion. Now, she helps other people make websites too. She is very happy with her job.

Even when she is busy, Mia takes a break to rest and eat a snack. Taking breaks helps her do a good job.

Later, Mia decided to work for a big company. Now, she meets more new people and has even more interesting projects. She continues to enjoy her work.
